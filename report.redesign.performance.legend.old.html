You will also see brackets with numbers in them under your results. This is your percentile ranking compared to others who completed that specific activity, given parallel versions are automatically embedded in the assessment to minimize learning effects.

Your results are used in calculating category scores for each section and your category scores are used to calculate an overall performance score. If you have stopped the assessment early or skipped activities within a category, a category score may not be able to be calculated and will appear blank or state 'Skipped'.

It is critical to interpret not only category scores but individual skills to understand your strengths and challenges. Your CBA or interpreting clinician will consider all of the information in the report and any other information available to them in making any recommendations for you.
